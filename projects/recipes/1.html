Beef Bowl Recipe

Meaning To Our Family

This is a recipe that we eat at least once a month. This is a good dinner menu, but it also works very well in lunch boxes.

Ingredients

warm water 400ml
shredded beef brisket 300g
onion 1
ginger 1 or 2 teaspoons
soy sause 4 tablespoons
rice wine 3 tablespoons
sugar 3 tablespoons
salt 1 teaspoon
tuna broth 2 teaspoons
