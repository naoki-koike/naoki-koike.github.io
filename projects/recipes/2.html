Stewed Hamburger Steak Recipe

Meaning To Our Family

This is my grandmother's recipe passed down to my mother, and we eat this quite often. My mother commented this as, When I cooked this recipe for my nephew recently, he recognised the taste as his grandmother's stewed hamburger steak.

Ingredients

"A" is the ingredients for the steak itself, and "B" is the ingredients for the sauce.
A is the amount for 1 person, but B is the amount for 5 people. Prep time is about an hour.

A
minced meat 70g
onion 35g
panko (breadcrums) 10g
milk 10ml
egg 12g
salt 1.3g
pepper 1 or 2 teaspoons
B
ketchup 1/2 cup
water 1 cup
worcestershire sauce 1 tablespoon
vinegar 2 tablespoons
salt 1/2 teaspoons
chicken bouillon 1 tablespoon

Instructions

  1. Mince the onions and pan-fry them.
  2. Immerse the panko with milk.
  3. Add all other ingredient in "A" and mix them.
  4. Make the steak shape and grill them until the steaks are golden brown.
  5. Mix all the ingredients in "B". Add the steaks into the sause and stew them to the inside, and you are finished!

Tips and Possibble Substitutions

  • Since the steak is stewed, it could be better if you leave it a day or two than just eating it as soon as you're done.
  • For the meat, it is typical to use beef, but you could mix with some pork if like that better.
  • Breadcrums can definitely substitute for panko if nowhere is selling them nearby.
